Love letters in small pieces

Hover to flip. Each one is a tiny truth.

I love the way your eyes soften when you’re thinking.
It’s when I know you’re dreaming out loud.
You make ordinary minutes feel cinematic.
Please never stop narrating our silly scenes.
Thank you for choosing me, especially on hard days.
I’ll keep choosing you, loudly and always.
Love, layered and luminous.
